The process

Four fixed stages. You always know which one you're on.

A‑1.01

Site assessment

Kate or a trained associate reviews your block, brief and budget against a set intake questionnaire — no open-ended meetings required to get started.

A‑1.02

Concept

You start from a proven template matched to your project type, adapted to your site. Fewer decisions from a blank page, same design judgement behind it.

A‑1.03

Feasibility

A fixed-scope review checks council, budget and buildability constraints before you spend on working drawings.

A‑1.04

Working plans

Final documentation is prepared at the level your package includes — from lodgement-ready plans to Kate's full bespoke set.
