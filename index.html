The pathway

Six fixed stages, from title check to occupancy. You always know which one you're on.

A‑1.01

Site & title checks

Confirm title, easements and existing conditions before you commit to a design brief.

A‑1.02

Zoning, overlays & planning permit

Work out what your block actually allows, and lodge for a planning permit where one's required.

A‑1.03

BPC Certificate of Consent

Complete the owner-builder learning module and secure your Certificate of Consent before construction can begin.

A‑1.04

Design, documentation & surveyor

Finalise your design brief and documentation, and engage a registered building surveyor.

A‑1.05

Staged construction & inspections

Move through each stage against a Victorian-timeline milestone schedule, with inspections booked at the right points.

A‑1.06

Occupancy & sign-off

Close out final inspections and secure your occupancy permit — the finish line.
